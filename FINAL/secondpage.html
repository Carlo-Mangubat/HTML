Pasay City South High School

Welcome to Pasay City South High School!

Pasay City South High School (PCSHS) is a public high school located at Piccio Garden, Villamor Air Base, Pasay City. Established in 1967, it serves students from grades 7 to 12, with an enrollment of over 5,000 students. The official website is pcshs.depedpasay.ph, and it is part of the Department of Education Pasay City division.

School Highlights

  • Academic programs: PCSHS offers programs for Junior High School (grades 7-10) and Senior High School (grades 11-12).
  • Extracurriculars: The school engages students in various activities such as sports, arts, and leadership programs. Recent events include a National Reading Month celebration and various science and nutrition-related contests.
  • Partnerships: The school has partnered with organizations like Telstra Philippines Foundation to enhance STEM students' research skills.
  • Awards: PCSHS has been recognized for its students' achievements, including ranking as a top P-TECH utilizers in NCR.
